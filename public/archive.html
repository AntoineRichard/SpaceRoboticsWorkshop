Summary

SMC-IT/SCC is a joint event composed of the International Conference on Space Mission Challenges for Information Technology (SMC-IT) and the Space Computing Conference (SCC). The joint conferences will focus on current systems practice and challenges as well as emerging hardware and software technologies with applicability for future space missions. Both conferences have a long history in the space community. Below you can get information about prior ocurrences of these events.
